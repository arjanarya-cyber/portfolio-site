About

I studied transportation design. At its core, that means designing spaces people have to live inside. Every cockpit, every cabin, every dashboard is a tiny world someone inhabits under pressure. That lens never left me.

Today I apply it to software. At GrowthX I own the mobile events platform end to end, from the moment a host decides to run an event to the moment an attendee walks in. Before that, I spent two years at AB InBev building AI tools and internal platforms for thousands of employees. One of them, Insights Copilot, grew from 100 users to 5,000 in a year. That kind of adoption does not happen by accident.

Something I actually believe

Most enterprise software is designed by people who never have to use it. I try to fix that. I run heatmaps, sit in feedback sessions, and look at drop-off data not because someone told me to, but because if I do not know where people get stuck, I am just guessing. The work I am most proud of is the kind the user never thought about. They just moved forward.
